The Mechanized Muttoneers, FRC Team 10266, is located at Hopkins School in New Haven, CT. The team is led by Ms. Connelly, and Captains: Albert Gan, Senior; Michael Illuzzi, Junior; Jeet Bhatnagar, Senior; Jacob Ceisler, Senior; and Wyatt Sloan, Senior. The Mechanized Muttoneers also have two FTC teams - Team 12377, and Team 13965 - which were created in 20XX. The Mechanized Muttoneers were created in 2024, through a student-led proposal by Jeet Bhatnagar, and Rohan Gottumukkala.

We are committed to offering a wide range of competitive and educational opportunities in the field of robotics to everyone at our school. Our goal is to foster a collaborative environment where everyone can enhance their skills, gain hands-on experience, and develop a deep understanding of robotics technology, regardless of their background or experience level.
