The Mechanized Muttoneers is a high school robotics located at Hopkins School in New Haven, CT. We strive to provide competition and learning opportunies in the field of robotics for all of our team members.

"Hopkins is a traditional, independent school of 710 students in grades 7–12. Located on a campus overlooking New Haven, the School takes pride in its distinguished faculty and a dedicated staff. We define ourselves as a community of civility and learning, one that educates students from diverse backgrounds to a full measure of their talents and humanity"

"FIRST® Robotics Competition teams design, program, and build a robot starting with a standard kit of parts and common set of rules to play in a themed head-to-head challenge. Teams also build a brand, develop community partnerships for support, and work to promote STEM in their local community."
